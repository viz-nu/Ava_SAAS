- Outbound campaigns with telephony integrations

Section 3: FAQ
Q: How do I upload documents?
A: Use getUploadUrl to obtain a presigned URL, upload to R2, then create a file collection with parserDetails.source_url.

Q: What file types are supported?
A: PDF, TXT (direct upload), and Office formats via LlamaParse (DOCX, XLSX, PPTX).
